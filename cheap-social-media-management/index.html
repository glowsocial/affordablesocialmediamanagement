The Hidden Cost of "Free" Social Media Tools

Most small business owners underestimate what DIY social media actually costs. Here's the math:

  • Writing 12 posts/month × 20 min each = 4 hours
  • Designing 12 images × 25 min each = 5 hours
  • Reformatting for each platform = 2 hours
  • Scheduling and publishing = 1 hour
  • Thinking about what to post next month = 2 hours

Total: 14–16 hours/month. At $75/hr for your time, that's $1,050–$1,200 in opportunity cost — for a social media presence that costs you $0 in software.

This is why the AI done-for-you category at $49–$99/month is genuinely the most cost-effective option for most small businesses. You're not just saving money on software. You're buying back 15 hours a month.
