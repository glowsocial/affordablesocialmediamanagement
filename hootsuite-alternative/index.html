The Best Hootsuite Alternatives for Small Businesses

Hootsuite charges $99+/month and still makes you write every post yourself. Here's what actually saves time — and what's worth paying for in 2026.

The Core Problem

Hootsuite is a scheduling tool, not a content creation tool. At $99+/month, you're paying for a dashboard that posts content on a schedule — but you still have to write every caption, design every image, and build every calendar yourself. For a small business owner, that's 15–20 hours a month in work that Hootsuite doesn't touch.

Why Scheduling-Only Tools Fall Short

The dirty secret of social media scheduling tools is that they solve the wrong problem. The hardest part of social media isn't posting on a schedule — it's coming up with something worth saying every week.

When you use Hootsuite, Buffer, or Later, here's what your workflow still looks like:

  • Brainstorm post ideas (30–60 min)
  • Write captions for each platform with the right length & tone (2–3 hrs)
  • Design images in Canva or source stock photos (2–4 hrs)
  • Resize for each platform's dimensions (30 min)
  • Upload everything and build the calendar (1–2 hrs)
  • Respond to comments and DMs (ongoing)

That's 6–10+ hours a month before you even open Hootsuite. The scheduling tool just handles the last click.

AI-powered done-for-you services like Glow Social eliminate everything on that list. The AI learns your business voice, writes the captions, generates the images, and publishes on your behalf. You spend 20 minutes reviewing and approving — not 10–15 hours creating.

Common Questions

What is the best Hootsuite alternative for small businesses?

For small businesses that don't have a content team, AI done-for-you services are the right category — not scheduling tools. Glow Social ($49/month) creates content in your brand voice and handles publishing. You just approve. Is there a free Hootsuite alternative?

Buffer has a free plan (3 channels, 10 posts each). Later has a free plan for Instagram only. Both are scheduling-only — you still write everything yourself. If you want actual content creation, there's no meaningful free option; AI done-for-you services start at $49/month.

Why did Hootsuite get so expensive?

Hootsuite raised prices significantly in 2023, killing their free plan and pushing the entry tier to $99/month. The backlash was significant enough that they temporarily walked it back, but pricing has remained high. Many small businesses found the jump unjustifiable given that the tool still doesn't create any content for you.
